Conference Overview

The 19th International Conference on the Foundations of Digital Games (FDG) proudly invites all research contributions advancing knowledge in the field of digital games; scholarly works in the disciplines of computer science, engineering, mathematics, natural sciences, humanities, social sciences, arts, and design are welcome. FDG aims to inspire critical discussion, collaboration, and ideation between academics of all backgrounds and disciplines within this diverse area of focus.

FDG invites contributions in the form of papers, games and demos, doctoral consortium applications, and proposals for panels, competitions, and workshops.

FDG 2024 is held in cooperation with a set of parties yet to be announced. Accepted papers will be published through the ACM International Conference Proceedings Series (ICPS), unless stated otherwise (see track information).
